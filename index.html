I am currently a Ph.D. student in School of Software, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Junhai Yong and Bin Wang.

My research interests center on computer vision and generative models, with a particular focus on diffusion models. Within this area, I work on efficient inference (TAP, DiffSparse, DiP-GO, TSS) and data augmentation (ReCon, DistDiff). Additionally, I explore vision-language models (SHIP) and fine-grained visual recognition (DCAL). I am expected to graduate in 2027.
